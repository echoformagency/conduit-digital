You win the client. You brief us.

Your agency closes the deal. You own the relationship. You set the strategic direction. You brief Conduit on the client, the objective, the budget, and the timeline. That’s the handoff. Everything from here runs under your brand.

What you hand over: a brief. What you keep: the relationship, the client, and the credit.

2
Conduit locks in the GPS before a dollar moves.

Before any campaign launches, Conduit defines KPAs with you, builds tracking infrastructure — GTM, GA4, CallRail, CRM integration — and aligns every channel element to a measurable business outcome. No campaign goes live without a defined goal and verified attribution.

What your client experiences: nothing yet. GPS is built before they see a single impression.

3
Your Pod runs everything. Daily. Invisibly.

Your dedicated Pod — the same senior U.S.-based specialists, every month — runs your campaigns daily against the defined KPAs. You get regular updates from your Pod Lead. Your clients get results they attribute to your agency. Conduit is completely invisible throughout.

What your client experiences: great results from their agency. They never know Conduit exists.

4
You walk in with proof. Renewals follow.

Monthly reporting delivered under your brand — CAC, LTV, MER, ROI connected to your client’s P&L. You present it. You take the credit. The renewal conversation stops being a negotiation and starts being a formality. The cycle repeats with the next client.

What your client experiences: the most credible reporting they’ve ever received from an agency.

The Invisibility Guarantee

Conduit doesn’t exist
as far as your clients are concerned.

This isn’t a setting you configure or a policy you enforce. It’s the structural foundation of how Conduit operates — because an agency-exclusive model only works if the agency stays the hero.

✗ Conduit never
✗ Contacts your clients directly — by email, phone, or any other channel
✗ Appears in CC or BCC on client-facing emails
✗ Produces client-facing reports with Conduit branding
✗ Attends client meetings or calls without explicit agency invitation
✗ Takes referrals from your clients to work with them directly
✗ Discloses the white-label relationship to your clients under any circumstances
✓ Conduit always
✓ Delivers all reports under your agency’s brand and formatting
✓ Communicates exclusively through your agency as the intermediary
✓ Uses your agency’s ad account credentials where required
✓ Builds campaign assets — ads, landing pages, creative briefs — in your client’s brand voice
✓ Attributes all results to your agency’s strategy in any client-facing material
✓ Operates as if Conduit is a division of your agency — not a vendor you hired

Why this is structural, not just policy: Conduit has never taken a direct client in 10+ years of operation. There is no direct-client revenue stream to protect. Agency-exclusive is not a sales positioning — it’s the only business model Conduit has ever operated.

What the Work Looks Like

Senior-level work means
you own the outcome.

At Conduit, there are no layers between you and the work. No analyst to delegate to. No account manager to translate your recommendations. You run the campaigns, you own the KPAs, and the results are yours.

Here’s what a typical week looks like for a specialist in a Pod:

The GPS standard you’ll hold:
✓ KPA defined before any campaign launches
✓ Tracking verified before any dollar moves
✓ Optimization decisions measured against the KPA — not the platform
✓ Monthly report in P&L language — not ad platform screenshots
Monday
Search term review + negative keyword build

Review previous week’s search term data against KPA performance. Build negative keyword additions from low-intent terms. Adjust bids where cost per KPA-conversion has shifted.

Tuesday
Creative rotation analysis

Review social creative performance by format and audience segment. Brief new creative direction where top performers are fatiguing. Update audience exclusions based on CRM match data.

Wednesday
KPA tracking verification

Weekly GTM and GA4 audit across active accounts. Verify CallRail call tracking is firing correctly. Check CRM integration for any conversion path breaks.

Thursday
Pod Lead weekly sync

Review KPA performance across all accounts with your Pod Lead. Flag anything not trending toward target before the agency asks. Plan the following week’s optimization priorities.

End of month
GPS report build + delivery

Build the monthly GPS report: CAC, ROAS, KPA performance vs. target, and optimization narrative. Delivered to the agency under their brand. Written in their client’s business language.

Always
Conduit stays invisible

Your work appears under the agency’s brand. Your clients’ clients never know Conduit exists. Your Pod Lead is your point of escalation — not the agency’s client.
